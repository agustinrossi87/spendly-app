Help topics

Here are some of the most common things users may need help with in Spendly.

How do I add a transaction?

Open the app, create a new entry, choose income or expense, then select an amount and category.

Can I organize transactions by category?

Yes. Spendly is built to help you review spending using categories like food, transport, rent, and more.

How do I review my monthly budget?

Go to your monthly overview to compare totals, see trends, and understand where your money is going.

The app is not saving correctly

Close and reopen the app first. If the issue continues, contact support with your device model and app version.

I found a bug

Please send a short description of the issue, the steps to reproduce it, and screenshots if available.

Need direct help?

Reach out by email and include as much detail as possible so support can help faster.
