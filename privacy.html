Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Spendly may collect information you provide directly in the app, such as:

  • Budget and transaction data you enter manually
  • Account-related information, if account features are offered
  • Support messages or feedback you send by email

The app may also collect limited technical information such as device type, app version, crash logs, or analytics data to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app’s features
  • Save and organize your budget data
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests and bug reports
  • Detect, prevent, or address technical issues

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the app, such as analytics, crash reporting, authentication, or cloud storage providers, only as needed to support the app’s functionality.

4. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

Depending on how the app works, you may be able to update or delete your data inside the app or by contacting us directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
